Banana Pudding

A dessert that will have people asking for the recipe is the following banana pudding. This combination of vanilla waffers, pudding, and freshly sliced bananas will not fail to impress anyone with a sweet tooth. The creaminess from the pudding and slight crunch from the vanilla waffers will have you wishing for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of vanilla waffers
  • 2/3 of a box of family size vanilla pudding
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1 small container of heavy whipping cream
  • cold water

Steps

  1. Make vanilla pudding. Mix 2/3 of the vanilla pudding package with 1 can of condensed milk. After adding the condensed milk use the can to measure equal parts of cold water and mix. Mix well and leave to chill for at least 2 hours.
  2. Make whip cream. Take the container of heavy whipping cream and pour into large bull. Whisk until soft peaks are achieved and leave to chill until vanilla pudding is done.
  3. Fold vanilla pudding into whip cream. After the time has passed fold the vanilla pudding into the whip cream.
  4. Prepare bananas. Slice 3 bananas into small slice which will be used to layer banana pudding.
  5. Assemble banana pudding. Into a container of choice layer the banana pudding. Start by laying a layer of vanilla pudding and adding slice or vanilla waffers. Lay another layer of vanilla pudding on top and repeat. Make sure to end with layer of banana pudding. Add top with crushed vanilla waffers.
