Chicken Fajitas

Making fajitas is one of the simplest and easiest recipes to make when in rush. A recipe that never fails to satisfy the pallet. A classic, juicy seasoned chicken, tossed with sauteed bell peppers and onions. To add even more flavor wrap in a warm torilla topped with avacado, cream cheese, or salsa. The possibilities are endless.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Seasoning of Choice

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken breast. Slice chicken into 1 inch cubes. (Make sure to cut as even as possible to ensure the chicken cooks evenly)
  2. Season the chicken. Generously add seasoning of choice on all side of the chicken cubes
  3. Cut onion and bell pepper Make sure they are cut into strips of similiar size to ensure even cooking
  4. Start cooking chicken. Into a skillet with oil of choice add seasoned chicken and leave until its fully cooked with a slight sear
  5. Sautee onions and bell peppers. Remove the chicken and add the onions and bell pepers to the same pan. Cook to preference.
  6. Combine everything. After the onions and bell peppers have been cooked to preference add chicken back into pan and enjoy the chicken fajitas.
